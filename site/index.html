Parking Options

Sunrise Ave has free street parking from 5 PM to 9 AM. The 200 block near Palm Beach Hotel is usually less crowded at night. Bradley Place has 2-hour parking, and Bradley Place north of Seminole/south of Royal Poinciana Way has free 1-hour parking during the day and free overnight from 5 PM to 10 AM. Paid parking is also available nearby.

Important Info

A few helpful reminders for a smooth Palm Beach stay.

House Rules

No unregistered guests or visitors. No pets. No loud music or parties. Smoking is absolutely prohibited in the unit. Please dispose of garbage properly.

Bathroom & Towels

Please feel free to use toiletries as needed. White washcloths, hand towels, bath towels, and bath mats are available for your use.

Emergency

In case of fire, exit the condo and follow the red exit signs. Do not use elevators. In an emergency, call 911 immediately.

Early Check-In / Late Check-Out

Requests are subject to availability and additional charges. Fees start at $50 and vary depending on hours requested.
